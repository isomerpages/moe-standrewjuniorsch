<h3>Register as Parent Volunteer Application (P1 Registration under Phase 2B)</h3>
<p>For parent/guardian that are interested to register themself to our school
as a parent volunteer not later than 1 July of the year before P1 registration
and has given at least 40 hours of voluntary service to the school by 30
June of the year of P1 registration.</p>
