<h3>School Uniform</h3>
<p>A saint must always wear his uniform with pride.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:60%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/attire.png">
</div>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are to wear the prescribed school uniform and modification to
the uniform is not allowed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The school uniform comprises:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Navy blue shorts</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>White shirt with blue school badge embroidered on the left-hand pocket
while the nametag must be on the right at the same level as badge</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Plain white shoes with laces and plain white socks which cover about 4
centimeters above the ankle</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p>Clean and tidy school uniform must be worn at all times.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>PE Attire</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Only School PE T-shirts and shorts of the standard design are allowed
during PE lessons and formal games. PE T-shirts must have the nametags
sewn on the right and always be neatly tucked in.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students may come to school in their PE attire on days that they have
PE lessons with the exception on Mondays where all students are expected
to be in the school uniform at the start of day.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are not required to change out of their PE attire after their
PE lessons.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>CCA T-shirts can only be worn on Fridays</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>On days where students have CCAs in the afternoon, they are still expected
to be in their school uniform during official school hours.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Appearance</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Student's hair must be in short and tidy. Hair must be above the ears,
collar and eyebrows.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Face is to be clean-shaven.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fancy hairstyle, dyeing and centre parting of hair are not allowed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fingernails must be short and clean.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Jewellery, fancy, colourful watches and items, or tinted spectacles must
not be worn to school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the interest of safety, charms, amulets, and other artefacts must not
be worn.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Conduct</h4>
<p>A Saint conducts himself well at all times and is a 'Servant Leader'.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are to be courteous and respectful to the Principal, Vice-Principal,
teachers, non-teaching staff and visitors to the school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be courteous and respectful to prefects and captains.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be considerate and courteous to the school mates</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be honest; surrender found items to the Lost and Found counter or teachers.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Love for Singapore</h4>
<p>A Saint takes the singing of the national anthem and recitation of the
national pledge seriously.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students who are Singapore citizens must sing the National Anthem and
take the pledge.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students will take the pledge with the right fist placed over the heart.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who are non-Singapore citizens must stand at attention and remain
respectful during assembly when the National Anthem is played, and the
Pledge is recited.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Love for the School</h4>
<p>A Saint love his school. As a member of the school community, a Saint
must</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Sing the school Hymn and school song.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>keep the school clean. e.g., return utensils after use, dispose rubbish
appropriately.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>use school facilities wisely, e.g., water, electricity and toilet paper.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>take good care of school property</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>report any damage or vandalism to school property immediately to the General
Office or teachers.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Love for Self and Others</h4>
<p>A Saint cares for others within the SAS(J) community. He will not only
look after his own interests, but also the interests of others. He cares
about:</p>
<h5>(i) Safety in the School</h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are not allowed to leave the school premises while waiting for
the school bus and before extra lessons or CCA.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Walk to your destination</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use school facilities in the way they are intended for.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be responsible for their schoolmates' as well as their own safety during
sports and games.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Report any injuries or accidents immediately to the General Office or
teachers.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h5>(ii) Responsibility in the School</h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are to be responsible for their belongings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Keep their belonging in their bag when they are not needed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure that their name and class are written or marked on their belonging.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Expensive items should not be brought to school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be punctual.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h5>(iii) Safety on the School Bus</h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are required to remain seated aboard the school bus.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be considerate - Do not distract the driver when he is driving.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>No part of their body must be positioned out of the window.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Report any injuries or accidents immediately to driver or assistants.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Obey the instructions of the bus drivers and assistants.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h5>(iv) Responsibility on the School Bus</h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are to be punctual - Do not keep the driver or schoolmates waiting.
Students are to inform the bus driver as soon as possible if they are not
taking the bus to school or home.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be courteous at all times. Greet and thank the driver and assistant.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cooperate with the bus captain.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be gracious and gentle. Speak softly and politely.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use bus facilities in the way they are intended for.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Keep the bus clean.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use the seat belts whenever they are available.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Homework Guidelines</h4>
<h5>(i) Purpose</h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Homework is a key aspect of formative assessment to provide ongoing and
valuable feedback for learning. All students should take their homework
seriously and complete it with pride</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h5>(ii) Guidelines</h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Homework may be issued for the March, June, September and/or December
holidays.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Homework issued will be stated on the class whiteboard. Students are reminded
to copy down the homework into their Personal Organiser.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h5>(iii) Workload</h5>
<p>On the average, homework issued each day would be:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Term Time: Weekly 7.5 hours per week / Not more than 1.5 hours per day</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Holidays: Weekly 5 hours per week / about 1 hour per day (for all subjects
added together)</p>
</li>
</ul>
